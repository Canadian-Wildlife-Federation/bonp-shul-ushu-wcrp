This WCRP was initiated in 2023. Since then, the passability of 71 structures has been assessed, and further habitat assessments were completed at 0 of these. Five priority barriers have been identified among the three watersheds.

Rehabilitated Barriers

No barriers were rehabilitated through the implementation of this WCRP as of May 2026.

Annual Progress Report

Since April 2025, the following actions were completed:

  • SFC and CWF presented the connectivity plan and process at the Salmonid Enhancement Program Community Workshop in Kamloops, B.C. in June 2025.
  • The first version of this WCRP was developed.
  • Barrier assessments were completed for 2 structures, resulting in 1 structure confirmed as a barrier and 1 structure excluded because there was no habitat upstream.
  • Owners were identified and contacted for 1 priority barrier.
  • The connectivity models were updated based on the above actions, and outputs were incorporated into this updated version of the WCRP.
